Affordable Products

Checkers is a well-known supermarket chain in South Africa that provides a wide range of grocery products to customers. It has over 200 stores across the country, including hypermarkets, supermarkets, and convenience stores. The hypermarkets are typically large retail outlets that offer a wide range of products, while the supermarkets are smaller and offer a more targeted selection of products. The convenience stores are often located in gas stations or at other high-traffic locations.

Checkers is a mass-market retailer that aims to provide quality products at affordable prices to consumers. Therefore, its strategic importance of the purchasing function is crucial to the profitability and prosperity of the supermarket. The purchasing function is responsible for sourcing the right products at the right price and making sure they are delivered on time. They need to maintain a balance between maintaining a competitive edge by keeping prices low while sourcing quality products that are in demand.
